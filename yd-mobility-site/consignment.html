Consignment

Sell your car with help, without doing all the work yourself.

What it means

Consignment means you keep ownership of your vehicle while we help market and sell it. We handle photos, listing, and communication. When it sells, you receive the agreed amount and we take a fee.

Photos & listing Messaging & scheduling Simple process

Basic steps

  1. Send your vehicle info and photos
  2. Agree on listing price
  3. We list and market the vehicle
  4. When sold, you get paid and we close the deal
